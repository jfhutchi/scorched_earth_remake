Tank Artillery Duel

v0.5.0

Destroy the opposing tank across a shifting battlefield of wind, craters, dirt mounds, and limited heavy ammo.

Controls

  • Left / Right Arrow adjust cannon angle
  • Up / Down Arrow adjust shot power
  • A / D move before firing
  • Spacebar fire
  • Tab or W cycle weapon
  • M mute or unmute
  • R restart current round
  • N next round after a win
  • Escape return to this menu

Weapons: Standard Shell makes a normal crater, Heavy Shell hits harder with a larger blast, and Dirt Bomb adds dirt to build mounds or fill craters.
