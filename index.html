Tank Artillery Duel

A 2-player turn-based artillery game

How to Play

  • Left / Right Arrow — adjust cannon angle
  • Up / Down Arrow — adjust shot power
  • Spacebar — fire projectile
  • R — restart match
  • Escape — return to main menu

Two tanks face off across procedurally generated terrain. Aim, account for wind, and blow up the other tank!
