Keyboard Controls

  • M mute or unmute

  • ◀ ▶ on the left move the tank

  • PWR- / PWR+ change shot power

  • WPN cycles weapons; ♪ mutes
  • R restarts; N goes to next round; ≡ returns to menu

Landscape is recommended. Default starting money is none, so earn cash through combat before shopping.

How to Play

Win the Duel

Set angle and power, account for wind, choose a weapon, and fire until the enemy tank is destroyed.

Match Length can be one round, Best of 3, or Best of 5. Best-of matches end when one player reaches the majority.

Damage and round wins earn money. The default match starts with $0, so the Round 1 shop is for planning.

Weapons

Standard Shell is unlimited. The catalog groups weapons into basic shells, precision, terrain, rolling, fire, split, and heavy explosive roles.

New v0.7.0 shop weapons include Precision Shell, Airburst Shell, Splitter Shell, Heavy Roller, Excavator Bomb, Mound Maker, and Firestorm Canister. Mega Bomb remains expensive and late-match.

Utilities

Shield absorbs damage. First Aid fully heals at the next round start if damaged. Parachute automatically reduces heavy fall damage after terrain collapses.

Controls

Desktop: Arrow keys aim and set power, A/D move, Space fires, Tab or W cycles weapons, M mutes, Esc returns to menu.

Mobile: use move, angle, PWR, FIRE, WPN, mute, and menu buttons. Landscape is the intended mobile layout.
