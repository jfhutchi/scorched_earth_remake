Tank Artillery Duel

v0.6.8

Destroy the opposing tank across a shifting battlefield of wind, craters, dirt mounds, rolling shells, flame patches, and limited special ammo.

Keyboard Controls

  • Left / Right Arrow adjust cannon angle
  • Up / Down Arrow adjust shot power
  • A / D move before firing
  • Spacebar fire
  • Tab or W cycle weapon
  • M mute or unmute
  • R restart current round
  • N next round after a win
  • Escape return to this menu

Weapons: Standard Shell is unlimited; limited ammo includes Heavy Shell, Dirt Bomb, Roller Shell, Napalm Canister, Cluster Bomb, and Mega Bomb.

Touch Controls

  • ◀ ▶ on the left move the tank
  • ↑ ↓ on the right adjust cannon angle
  • PWR- / PWR+ change shot power
  • FIRE launches one shot per tap
  • WPN cycles weapons; ♪ mutes
  • R restarts; N goes to next round; ≡ returns to menu

Landscape is recommended for the clearest aim.
